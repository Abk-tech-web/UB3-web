About UB3

A trusted home base for people navigating Web3

UB3 (Unbound DAO 3) exists to make Web3 make sense. We bring together research, education, and real opportunities so builders, learners, and organizations can move through the decentralized economy with confidence instead of guesswork.

Whether you're taking your first steps into blockchain or already building inside it, UB3 gives you trusted insights, structured learning, an active community, and pathways into real roles and rewards.

Where We're Headed

Roadmap

UB3 is built to expand — from research and education today, to governance and Web3 utilities ahead.

Phase 01 — Foundation

Research, Education & Community

Publish trusted research, launch beginner-to-advanced education, and grow an active, moderated community.

Phase 02 — Opportunity

Ambassador, Career & Grant Programs

Open ambassador programs, job placements, internships, grants, bounties, and hackathons for the community.

Phase 03 — Platform

Leadership & Member Portals

Ship the secure leadership management platform and expand into member accounts and dashboards.

Phase 04 — Governance

DAO Governance & Web3 Utilities

Introduce token utilities, NFT utilities, and on-chain governance and voting for the UB3 community.
